FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

How fast do I get access after paying?

Usually within a few hours, always within 24 hours. You'll get an email with all links for your tier.

Which payment methods do you accept?

PayPal, card payments, Ko‑fi, and direct bank transfer. If none of these work for you, email me and we'll figure something out.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Memberships are month to month — just stop the payment and your access ends at the close of the paid month.

I missed a live webinar. Can I watch it later?

Member and Pro tiers include the full recordings archive, so you can catch up anytime.
